# Product Manager Skills Guide

Successful Product Managers combine business, technology, and leadership skills.

## Core Product Management Skills

### Product Strategy

Develop long-term product vision and business alignment.

### Customer Research

Understand customer needs through interviews, surveys, and feedback analysis.

### Prioritization

Evaluate opportunities using frameworks such as RICE and MoSCoW.

### Roadmapping

Plan product initiatives and communicate priorities.

### Data Analysis

Use metrics and customer insights to guide decisions.

### Stakeholder Management

Collaborate effectively with engineering, design, marketing, and leadership teams.

### Communication

Clearly communicate product vision, requirements, and outcomes.

## Technical Skills for Product Managers

- Agile Methodologies
- Product Analytics
- A/B Testing
- User Story Writing
- Product Requirements Documentation

## Leadership Skills

- Decision Making
- Strategic Thinking
- Conflict Resolution
- Team Collaboration
