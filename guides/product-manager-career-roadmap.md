# Product Manager Career Roadmap

A structured roadmap for professionals who want to build a successful career in Product Management.

## Stage 1: Build Product Foundations

Learn:

- Product Management Fundamentals
- Customer Research
- Product Discovery
- Market Research
- Product Strategy

## Stage 2: Learn Product Execution

Develop expertise in:

- Product Roadmapping
- Agile Product Management
- Stakeholder Management
- Product Analytics
- Prioritization Frameworks

## Stage 3: Build Real-World Experience

Work on:

- Product Requirement Documents (PRDs)
- User Stories
- Product Launches
- Product Metrics
- Customer Feedback Analysis

## Stage 4: Grow into Leadership

Develop skills in:

- Product Vision
- Team Leadership
- Cross-functional Collaboration
- Strategic Decision Making

## Recommended Skills

- Product Strategy
- User Research
- Product Discovery
- Data Analysis
- Communication
- Leadership
