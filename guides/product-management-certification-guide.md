# Product Management Certification Guide

Product Management certifications help professionals build practical skills in product strategy, product discovery, prioritization, and product leadership.

## Why Pursue Product Management Certification?

Benefits include:

- Structured learning
- Industry-recognized credentials
- Hands-on projects
- Career advancement opportunities
- Product leadership development

## Skills Covered

### Product Strategy

Learn how to define product vision and align business objectives.

### Product Discovery

Identify customer problems and validate solutions.

### Product Roadmapping

Plan product initiatives and communicate priorities.

### Agile Product Management

Work effectively with engineering and design teams.

### Product Analytics

Measure outcomes using data-driven decision making.

## Who Should Enroll?

- Aspiring Product Managers
- Product Owners
- Business Analysts
- Software Professionals
- Entrepreneurs
