# Product Manager Interview Guide

Preparing for a Product Manager interview requires a combination of product thinking, business strategy, and communication skills.

## Common Product Manager Interview Topics

### Product Design

Examples:

- Design a product for students.
- Improve a food delivery application.
- Design a new feature for a social media platform.

### Product Strategy

Examples:

- How would you grow a SaaS product?
- How would you enter a new market?

### Metrics

Examples:

- What metrics would you track for a product?
- How would you measure feature success?

### Prioritization

Examples:

- Explain the RICE Framework.
- How do you prioritize competing requests?

## Product Manager Interview Framework

### Understand the Problem

Ask clarifying questions.

### Identify Users

Understand the target audience.

### Define Success

Determine measurable outcomes.

### Generate Solutions

Explore multiple approaches.

### Evaluate Trade-offs

Consider risks and benefits.

## Frequently Asked Product Manager Interview Questions

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why Product Management?
3. Describe a product you admire.
4. How do you prioritize features?
5. Tell me about a challenging stakeholder situation.

## Skills Interviewers Look For

- Product Thinking
- Analytical Skills
- Communication
- Leadership
- Customer Empathy
