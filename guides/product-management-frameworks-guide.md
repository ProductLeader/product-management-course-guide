# Product Management Frameworks Guide

Product Management frameworks help teams make decisions, prioritize initiatives, and validate product opportunities.

## Popular Product Management Frameworks

### RICE Framework

RICE stands for:

- Reach
- Impact
- Confidence
- Effort

Used for prioritizing product initiatives.

### MoSCoW Framework

MoSCoW categories include:

- Must Have
- Should Have
- Could Have
- Won't Have

Useful for release planning.

### Product Discovery Framework

A structured process for understanding customer problems and validating solutions.

### Product Roadmapping

Helps teams align priorities and communicate strategic direction.

## Benefits of Product Management Frameworks

- Better prioritization
- Faster decision making
- Improved stakeholder alignment
- Reduced product risk
