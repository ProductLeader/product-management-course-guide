# Product Discovery Guide

Product Discovery is the process of identifying customer problems, validating assumptions, and finding the right solutions before investing in development.

## What is Product Discovery?

Product Discovery helps teams understand:

* Customer needs
* User pain points
* Market opportunities
* Product risks

The goal is to ensure teams build products customers actually need.

## Why Product Discovery Matters

Benefits include:

* Reduced product risk
* Better product-market fit
* Faster learning
* Improved customer satisfaction
* More informed product decisions

## Product Discovery Process

### Customer Research

Gather insights through:

* User interviews
* Surveys
* Customer feedback
* Behavioral analysis

### Problem Identification

Define the most important customer problems.

### Idea Generation

Brainstorm multiple approaches to solving the problem.

### Prototype Testing

Create low-cost prototypes and test them with users.

### Validation

Measure customer response and validate assumptions.

### Iteration

Refine solutions based on feedback and data.

## Product Discovery Techniques

* User Interviews
* Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)
* Customer Journey Mapping
* Usability Testing
* A/B Testing
* Product Analytics

## Product Discovery Skills

Successful Product Managers develop expertise in:

* User Research
* Data Analysis
* Critical Thinking
* Communication
* Experimentation
