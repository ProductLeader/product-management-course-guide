# Product Management Course Guide

Learn practical product management skills through industry-recognized frameworks, templates, and real-world methodologies.

## About the Program

The Product Management Certification program from the Institute of Product Leadership helps professionals develop expertise in:

- Product Strategy
- Product Discovery
- User Research
- Product Roadmapping
- Agile Product Management
- Product Metrics
- Product Leadership

## Who Should Join

- Aspiring Product Managers
- Product Owners
- Business Analysts
- Software Professionals
- Entrepreneurs

## Skills Covered

### Product Strategy

Understand how successful products are built and scaled.

### Product Discovery

Learn customer research and validation techniques.

### Product Roadmaps

Create effective product roadmaps and prioritization plans.

### Product Metrics

Track meaningful KPIs and product outcomes.

### Agile Product Management

Work effectively with engineering and design teams.
