# Product Discovery Framework

Product Discovery helps teams identify customer problems before building solutions.

## What is Product Discovery?

Product Discovery is the process of understanding customer needs, validating assumptions, and identifying opportunities.

## Key Stages

### Customer Research

Methods include:

- User interviews
- Surveys
- Observation
- Feedback analysis

### Problem Validation

Determine whether the problem is important enough to solve.

### Solution Exploration

Generate multiple solutions before selecting one.

### Prototype Testing

Test concepts with real users.

### Learning and Iteration

Continuously refine based on user feedback.

## Benefits

- Reduced product risk
- Better customer outcomes
- Faster learning
- Improved product-market fit
